Delete Your Data

Paint Aloha respects your privacy and gives you control over your data. You can request deletion of any data associated with your use of our app or services.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please email us at:

paintaloha.orders@gmail.com

What to Include in Your Request

  • Your email address (if you placed an order)
  • Any order confirmation numbers
  • Description of data you want deleted

What Data We May Have

  • Order information: Name, email, shipping address (if you ordered a kit)
  • Photos: Images uploaded for kit orders
  • Payment info: Handled securely by Stripe (not stored by us)

Data Retention

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:

  • Delete your order information within 30 days
  • Remove any uploaded photos from our servers
  • Confirm deletion via email

Note: We may retain certain data as required by law (e.g., financial records for tax purposes).
