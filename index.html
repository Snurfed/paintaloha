Frequently Asked Questions

How does Paint Aloha work?

Simply upload any photo through our free app or website. We transform it into a numbered canvas with matched acrylic paints. You receive everything needed to paint your masterpiece - canvas, paints, brushes, and a color guide.

What size canvas do I get?

Each kit includes a 5x7 inch canvas with your custom design. This size is perfect for framing and display on desks, shelves, or walls.

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 5-7 business days. Shipping is free anywhere in the United States.

What photos work best for paint by numbers?

Photos with good contrast and clear subjects work best. Pet portraits, family photos, landscapes, and travel memories are popular choices. Our app automatically optimizes your photo for the best painting result.

Is Paint Aloha made in Hawaii?

Yes! Every Paint Aloha kit is handmade with aloha in Honolulu, Hawaii. We take pride in creating quality products that bring joy to our customers.
