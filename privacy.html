Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Overview

Paint Aloha ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile app and order paint kits.

Information We Collect

Photos: When you use our app, you select photos from your device. These photos are processed locally on your device to generate your paint-by-numbers canvas. We do not upload or store your photos on any server.

Order Information: When you order a kit, we collect your name, shipping address, and email to fulfill your order. This information is only used to process and ship your order.

Payment Information: Payments are processed through secure third-party providers (Stripe, Venmo, Zelle). We do not store your payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process and ship your paint kit orders
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your order (e.g., shipping carriers).

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All payment processing is handled by secure, PCI-compliant providers.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your order data by contacting us at paintaloha.orders@gmail.com.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at paintaloha.orders@gmail.com.
